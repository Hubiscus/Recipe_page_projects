Curry Chicken Recipe

Chicken with a mix of local Jamican Spices usually served with whit rice or rice and beans

Ingridents

  • Chicken
  • Onions, Green Oninions, Bell Peppers , and tomatoes . (Diced)
  • Garlic, ginger, cilantro and fresh thyme, all chopped.

  • Spices
  • Potatoes

Steps:

  1. Toast spices
  2. Chop & season chicken breasts
  3. Cover chicken with coconut milk to marinate a few minutes
  4. Chop & cook onion, jalapeno, and ginger/garlic
  5. Add lemon juice, add chicken in coconut milk, add broth
  6. Thicken sauce & simmer. Then EAT!
